Open product questions decide

  1. 3-up KPI grid: Doc says 3-up, but with 4 KPIs we render 2×2 on a 390px screen. Drop one? Or stack 1+3?
  2. "Delta down = good": drinking less is good but visually down often reads as bad. Preview reverses color (down=green, up=amber). Keep, or use ↓ + green pill consistently?
  3. Hero KPI: Should "alcohol-free days" be visually elevated (full-width, celebratory tone) since it's the most affirmative metric? Preview tones it celebratory but keeps grid size.
  4. Footer copy: tone-adaptive ("you're inside your usual range", "nice and steady", "your alcohol-free days are climbing") vs. static reassurance.
  5. Nav entry: bottom tab vs. Profile entry — doc §11 Q1 still open.
